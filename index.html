I am a Research Engineer at Inception Institute of Artificial Intelligence, Abu Dhabi. I am primarily interested in deep learning, at the intersection of Speech & Audio processing, Computer Vision and natural language processing. Before my current position, I completed my bachelor's in Computer Science and Engineering from DIT University, Dehradun with a semester exchange at Indian Institute of Technology, Roorkee supervised by [Dr R Balasubramanian](). During my undergraduate studies, In summers 2017, I was selected as a Machine Learning Intern at Indian Institute of Technology, Roorkee supervised by Dr R Balasubramanian and in summers 2018 as an Outreachy student, with Mozilla Organisation supervised by [Emma Irvin](). I was also an active member of Deeplearn-lab, open source research collaboration platform headed by [Mr Gaurav Bhatt]().
